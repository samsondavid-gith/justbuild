Build Your Own

From thinking about AI to building with it.

Most professionals talk about AI. Some use it. Very few build with it. Build Your Own turns you into the third kind.

Not through a course. Not through a certificate. Through actually building something real with your own idea, your own hands, real AI tools and putting it in the world.

Who this is for

Working professionals across functions. Early to mid-career. Any background. You do not need to write code.

The new generation of AI tools is the first that can be operated entirely in plain language. Coding is not the wall anymore. The wall between having an idea and shipping a working version of it has dropped. This is for everyone that wall used to keep out.

What this is

A two-day in-person weekend. Four weeks of online build support. Demo Day.

The weekend gives you momentum. The next four weeks make it real.

How it works

A weekend kickoff. Four weeks of structured build support. Demo Day.

The weekend gets you from idea to your first working version. By Sunday evening you have something real that did not exist on Friday. Built with your own hands.

The four weeks after are when the rough first version becomes something you can demonstrate at work, in interviews, in pitches. Structured online support every week. Real users in the loop. By the end, you present what you built and where it goes next.

Two paths forward

Most professionals use Build Your Own to become harder to replace in their current job. Some turn it into something of their own.

Path One — Career edge. You walk into your next promotion conversation, your next interview, your next pitch with working AI you can demonstrate. Not a claim. Proof.

Path Two — Your own venture. Some professionals turn the build into the start of their own thing. The side project that becomes the bridge out. Or the thing that quietly grows alongside the day job.

One workshop. Two paths forward.
