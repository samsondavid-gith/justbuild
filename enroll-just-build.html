    Just Build

    Stop learning AI. Start building with it.

    Most people talk about AI. Some people use it. Very few people build with it. Just Build turns you into the third kind.

    Not through a course. Not through a certificate. Through actually building something real with your own idea, your own hands, real AI tools and putting it in the world.

    Who this is for

    College students. Undergraduate or postgraduate. Any discipline. You do not need to write code. You do not need a technical background.

    The new generation of AI tools is the first that can be operated entirely in plain language. The wall between having an idea and shipping a working version of it has dropped. This is built for everyone that wall used to keep out.

    What this is

    A two-day in-person weekend. Four weeks of online build support. Demo Day.

    The weekend gives you momentum. The next four weeks make it real.

    Two tracks

    Same build. Two destinations.

    Same weekend. Same build month. Same intellectual spine. The track you pick shapes what you build, and what you do with it after.

    Foundation

    Built for placements.

    Build a working AI portfolio piece. By placement season, when the recruiter asks "show me what you can do with AI," your answer is already in your hands.

    Ventures

    Built for your own venture.

    Pick from a curated palette of twenty-four buildable ideas, or bring your own. Leave with a real working venture you can keep building.

    You pick a track when you apply. Not sure yet? Tell us. We'll help you decide.

    How it works

    A weekend kickoff. Four weeks of structured build support. Demo Day.

    The weekend gets you from idea to your first working version. By Sunday evening you have something real, a live AI app, that did not exist on Friday.

    The four weeks after are when the rough first version becomes something you would be proud to show. Structured online support every week. Real users in the loop. By the end, you present what you built and where it goes next.
