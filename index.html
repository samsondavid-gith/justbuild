Just Build

Stop learning AI. Start building with it.

Most people talk about AI. Some people use it. Very few people build with it. Just Build turns you into the third kind.

Not through a course. Not through a certificate. Through actually building something real with your own idea, your own hands, real AI tools and putting it in the world.

Who this is for

Non-engineers. You do not need to write code. You do not need a technical background.

The new generation of AI tools is the first that can be operated entirely in plain language. The wall between having an idea and shipping a working version of it has dropped. This is built for everyone that wall used to keep out.

What this is

A two-day weekend, either in person or online via Zoom, followed by four weeks of structured online build support.

The weekend gives you momentum. The next four weeks make it real.

How it works

Phase 1 — The Build Weekend

Day 1 morning — watch a complete AI product built live from scratch. Idea to working prototype. Every decision visible. Every tool real.

Day 1 afternoon and Day 2 — you build your own. Your idea. Your problem. Your user.

By Sunday evening you have a working prototype, a live URL, and your first version in the world.

Phase 2 — The Build Month

Four weeks of structured support to take your build from prototype to something real.

Week 1 — Refine. Improve based on specific feedback.

Week 2 — Validate. Talk to real users. Not friends.

Week 3 — Iterate. Change something substantial.

Week 4 — Launch. Demo day. Present your build, what changed, and what comes next.

How you think while building

Everything runs on one lens.

Humanics. Mechanics. Valuenomics.

Who is this for.

What is AI actually doing.

Why would anyone pay.

Not taught as theory. Applied at every decision. By the end it becomes how you think. Not just for this build but for every one after.

What you leave with

A real build

A working product with a live URL you can open and show in seconds.

The Just Build Toolkit

Frameworks, templates, prompts, and guides. Ready to use on your next build.

The OC Certificate

Awarded at the end of the Build Month. It marks completion. Not attendance.

A repeatable way to build

You now know how to go from idea to reality. Again and again.

The confidence that comes from doing

You know from experience that you can start from zero and create something real.

What continues after

Just Build does not end when the program ends.

OC Alumni Network

A long-term community of builders. Your cohort becomes your first real network.

Internship opportunities at Orange Caterpillar

For students and early career participants. A chance to build inside OC.

The David Foundation Scholarship

25 percent of fees returned to the most honest build in the cohort. Not the prettiest. The one that understood a real person and built something real for them.

OC Ventures

Select builds with real signal are taken forward. With support, network, and guidance.

The one thing

If everything reduces to one outcome it is this.

You leave as someone who builds with AI.

Not someone who attended a program. Not someone who learned tools. Someone who has proof.

Something real on your laptop that did not exist before.

Everything else is in service of that.
