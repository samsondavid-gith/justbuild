How it works

Your tree, from blossom to box.

No farm to visit, no work to do. You choose, we grow, you receive.

01

You choose your tree

Pick a fruit and a farm in the app. Your tree is tagged with your name for the whole season, so it is truly yours.

02

We tend it all season

Our farmers water, prune and protect it the way they have for years. You get the harvest without lifting a finger.

03

It flowers and fruits

You get photo updates as your tree wakes up, blossoms and slowly fills with fruit. The waiting is half the joy.

04

Picked, packed, shipped

At peak ripeness we hand pick your tree's harvest, pack it to travel well, and ship it to your door.

Questions

Questions, answered.

Is it really my tree?+
Yes. A real tree on a real farm, tagged with your name for the season. You get that tree's fruit, not a random box from a warehouse.
What if the harvest is small?+
Nature varies, and we are honest about that. If a tree does badly through no fault of yours, we make it right.
Which fruits can I get?+
It depends on the season and the farm. Mango, guava, sweet lime, pomegranate and more appear through the year. The app shows what is available now.
How is it shipped?+
Hand picked at ripeness, packed to travel well, and shipped to your door across most of India.
Can I gift a tree?+
Yes. Adopt a tree in someone's name and the harvest goes to them, with a note from you.
