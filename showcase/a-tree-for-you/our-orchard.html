Our orchard

Real farms. Real trees. Real seasons.

A Tree for You started with a simple wish: that the fruit you eat could come from a tree you actually know.

So we partner with small farms and tag real trees with real names. When your tree fruits, that is the fruit you get. Not a warehouse pick, not a blend from ten farms.

The farmers do what they have always done, growing the slow honest way. Your rent reaches them directly, and a season later it reaches you as fruit.
