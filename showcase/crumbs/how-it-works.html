How it works

From our oven to your table.

See what is baking, place your order, and get it fresh by pickup or nearby delivery.

01

See today's bakes

Open the order pad and see what is fresh today, breads, cakes, pastries and little treats. The menu changes with the day.

02

Place your order

Pick your bakes. For a cake, add the date, size and message. Tell us pickup or delivery, and that is it.

03

We bake it fresh

Your order is baked the same day, in small batches, never pulled off an old shelf.

04

Pick up or delivery

Collect it warm from the bakery, or we drop it to you nearby the same day.

Questions

Questions, answered.

Do you make eggless cakes?+
Yes. Most of our cakes and bakes can be made eggless. Just choose eggless in the order pad when you order.
How much notice do you need for a custom cake?+
A day or two is ideal for a custom cake, more for bigger or tiered ones. The order pad shows the soonest date we can do.
Do you deliver?+
Yes, nearby. Collect it warm from the bakery, or we drop it close by the same day. Check your area in the pad.
Can I order daily bread?+
Yes. Add your breads to the order pad and pick them up fresh, or set a regular order.
How do I pay?+
Pay on pickup or delivery, by cash or UPI. Simple, no app store needed.
