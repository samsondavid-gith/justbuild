Our bakery

Flour, butter, and a hot oven by 5 am.

Crumbs is a small neighbourhood bakery that bakes everything fresh, every morning, for the people on its street.

No factory trays trucked in overnight. We mix, prove and bake right here, and the order pad keeps it simple: you see what is fresh, you order, we bake.

A birthday cake for Saturday? Tell us today. Daily bread for the week? It is in the pad. Real baking, made to order, close to home.
