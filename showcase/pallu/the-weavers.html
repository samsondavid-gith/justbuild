The weavers

Real hands,
real looms.

Pallu exists to put the maker back in the picture. Here is what we promise on every saree we sell.

01

Pure handloom, named

Every saree tells you its weave and its region, and whether it is handloom or not. No powerloom sold as handloom.

02

A fair share to the weaver

We buy direct from weaving clusters and show what share of the saree cost reaches the weaver. The maker is paid fairly, not squeezed.

03

A blouse that actually fits

Send your measurements and we stitch a made-to-measure blouse. If the fit is not right, the first alteration is free.

04

A price with nothing hidden

Saree, drape, blouse stitching and tax, each shown on its own line. You always know exactly what you are paying for.

Why it matters

A saree should carry a story you are proud to wear, and a price you are proud to pay.
