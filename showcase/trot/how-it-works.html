How it works

From "oh no"
to "all good".

A busy day should not mean a dog stuck indoors. Here is exactly how a Trot walk works, start to finish.

Step 1

Book a vetted walker.

Open the app, pick a time, and choose a vetted walker near you. Need one right now? Book on demand and a walker is on the way. Save a favourite walker for next time.

Step 2

Follow the walk live.

The moment the walk starts you get a ping. Open the live map and watch the full route in real time, with distance and walk time updating as they go.

Step 3

Get a photo report.

When your dog is home safe you get a report card: a fresh photo, the route, the distance, the time, and whether they did their business. Plus a friendly note from the walker.

Always

Safe, every walk.

Every walker is background checked and trained, and every walk is insured. If anything ever feels off, our team is one tap away.
