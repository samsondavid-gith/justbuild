Our walkers

Handing your dog to someone else is a big deal. We take it as seriously as you do. Here is how every Trot walker earns that trust.

Background checked

Every walker is ID verified and background checked before they take a single leash. No shortcuts.

Trained in dog care

Walkers are trained in safe handling, leash etiquette and basic first aid, so your dog is in capable hands.

Tracked, every walk

Every walk is followed live on the map and recorded, so there is always a clear, honest record of where your dog went.

Rated by pet parents

You see real ratings and reviews from other pet parents, and you can request the same walker again and again.
