Our promise

Trust, in writing.

Zevar exists for one reason: buying fine jewellery should not feel like a gamble. Here is exactly what we stand behind.

01

A live, itemised price

Every piece breaks down the gold weight times the current rate, the making charge, the stone value and the tax. You see the full sum before you buy, and the gold rate updates live.

02

BIS hallmarked gold

All our gold is BIS hallmarked and carries a unique HUID code. You can verify it independently, so purity is never a question of trust.

03

IGI certified diamonds

Every diamond ships with its IGI certificate stating cut, colour, clarity and carat. No vague grades, no inflated claims.

04

Lifetime buyback

Bring any Zevar piece back, any time, and exchange it at fair, transparent value based on the day's gold rate. Your jewellery keeps its worth.

We would rather you understand the price completely than be dazzled by it briefly.
